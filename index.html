About Us

At TechOptics, we are dedicated to transforming diabetic retinopathy care with our cutting-edge retinal imaging camera and advanced machine learning model. Our mission is to detect and manage diabetic retinopathy early, preventing vision loss and improving quality of life.

Our Technology

Retinal Imaging Camera

Our state-of-the-art retinal imaging camera captures high-resolution images of the retina, enabling accurate diagnosis of diabetic retinopathy.

Machine Learning Model

Our advanced machine learning model analyzes retinal images to detect early signs of diabetic retinopathy, ensuring timely intervention and treatment.
